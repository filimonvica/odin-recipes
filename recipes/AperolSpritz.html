Aperol Spritz

Odin's favourite drink when in Italy

Get a taste of summer with this classic Italian cocktail of Aperol, prosecco and soda.

Eyeball ingredients or try our proportions.

Ingredients

  • ice
  • 100ml Aperol
  • prosecco
  • soda, to top up

Steps

  1. Put a couple of cubes of ice into 2 glasses.
  2. Add a 50 ml measure of Aperol to each.
  3. Divide the prosecco between the glasses and then top up with soda, if you like.
