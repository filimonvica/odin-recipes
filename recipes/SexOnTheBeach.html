Sex on the Beach

Odin's first sex...on the beach

Combine vodka with peach schnapps and cranberry juice to make a classic sex on the beach cocktail.

Garnish with cocktail cherries and orange slices.

Ingredients

  • ice
  • 50ml vodka
  • 25ml peach schnapps
  • 2 oranges, juiced, plus 2 sliced to garnish
  • 50ml cranberry juice
  • glace cherries, to garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Fill two tall glasses with ice cubes.
  2. Pour the vodka, peach schnapps and fruit juices into a large jug and stir.
  3. Divide the mixture between the two glasses and stir gently to combine.
  4. Garnish with the cocktail cherries and orange slices.
